## Quick Start for Students

1. **Sign Up** with your university email
2. **Verify** your email with the OTP code
3. **Complete Your Profile** with your department and batch
4. **Start Exploring** - Browse marketplace, rent items, or join conversations!

## Who Can Use EduSync?

| User Type | What You Can Do |
|-----------|----------------|
| **Students** | Buy, sell, rent, chat, post news, report issues |
| **Vendors** | Run your shop, manage products, process orders |
| **Admins** | Manage users, moderate content, resolve issues |
