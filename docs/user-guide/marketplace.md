## How to Use the Marketplace

### Buying Products

1. **Browse** - Click on "Marketplace" in the navigation
2. **Choose a Category**:
   - Foods - Browse food vendors
   - Shops - Browse startup vendors
   - Pre-Owned - Browse used items
3. **View Details** - Click on any product to see full description, price, and seller info
4. **Add to Cart** - Click "Add to Cart" button
5. **Checkout** - Review your cart and place order
6. **Pay** - Complete payment through SSLCommerz (secure payment gateway)

### Selling Your Items

1. **Go to "Sell"** section
2. **Fill in Details**:
   - Title (e.g., "CSE 101 Textbook - Like New")
   - Description (condition, reason for selling)
   - Price (be fair and competitive!)
   - Category (Books, Electronics, etc.)
   - Upload clear photos
3. **Publish** - Your listing goes live immediately
4. **Wait for Buyers** - You'll get notifications when someone's interested
5. **Complete Sale** - Coordinate with buyer via chat
